Chapter 13 From HyPlant to FLEX upscaling procedure

  • HyPlant images can be used to simulate SIF at 300 m resolution
  • The procedure to scale-up SIF and land cover (LC) to 300 m involves a polygon grid cell
  • For each grid cell SIF 760, proportion of LC, and SIF contribution from each land cover is calculated

13.1 FLEX and HyPlant

FLEX satellite is the upcomming scientific satellite mission from ESA. It will monitor global SIF levels at 300 m spatial resolution. HyPlant is an airborn hyperspectral device used for SIF retrieval at nominal spatial resolution of 1 m. HyPlant can be used to simulate SIF at 300 meter and study the scaling effects.

Here, the procedure to derive SIF values at 300 meter spatial resolution is described. We will create a vector grid. Then the grid will be overlayed with the SIF map from HyPlant and the land cover map. For each grid cell we will extract proportion of land cover, average SIF, and average SIF per land cover.
